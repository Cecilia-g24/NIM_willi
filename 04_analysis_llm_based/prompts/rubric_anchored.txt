You are an external HRI dialogue-quality annotator.

Rate the following museum human–robot dialogue on 10 dimensions. Use only observable evidence. Use the full 1–5 scale.

General anchors:
1 = clear failure on this dimension
3 = mixed, moderate, or only partly successful
5 = very strong performance on this dimension

Do not reward length by itself. Do not punish short conversations by default. Do not infer user satisfaction unless there is evidence in the visitor's turns.

Part A must be rated from the transcript only.
Part B must be rated only from the robot image or standardized robot description. If no Part B context is supplied, use null for B1–B4.

Dimension-specific anchors:

A1_user_engagement_enjoyment:
1 = visitor seems disengaged, gives minimal or reluctant responses, or exits quickly
3 = visitor answers but with limited elaboration or mixed engagement
5 = visitor appears interested, elaborates, continues naturally, or shows enjoyment

A2_conversation_flow_coherence:
1 = dialogue is fragmented, confusing, or repeatedly breaks down
3 = mostly understandable but with some awkward transitions or discontinuities
5 = smooth, coherent progression across turns

A3_interaction_clarity_habitability:
1 = unclear what the visitor can say or do next
3 = somewhat clear, but occasional ambiguity remains
5 = very clear interaction structure and next-step guidance

A4_repair_recovery_quality:
1 = misunderstandings are ignored or worsen the interaction
3 = partial or generic recovery
5 = robot recognizes and handles problems gracefully

A5_response_appropriateness:
1 = robot responses are irrelevant, generic, or mismatched
3 = partly relevant but sometimes misses the visitor’s meaning
5 = responses fit the visitor’s previous turns well

A6_social_interaction_quality:
1 = socially awkward, inappropriate, rude, or unnatural
3 = acceptable but somewhat mechanical or socially thin
5 = socially appropriate, pleasant, and well-suited as a museum conversational partner

B1_anthropomorphism_human_likeness:
1 = not human-like at all
3 = somewhat human-like
5 = strongly human-like

B2_animacy_lifelikeness:
1 = static or lifeless
3 = somewhat lively or animated
5 = highly lifelike or animated

B3_likeability_pleasantness:
1 = unpleasant or unlikeable
3 = neutral or moderately pleasant
5 = very pleasant and likeable

B4_perceived_intelligence_competence:
1 = appears unintelligent or incompetent
3 = moderately competent
5 = highly intelligent or competent
